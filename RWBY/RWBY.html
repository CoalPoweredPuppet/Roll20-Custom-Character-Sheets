Statistics


Health

 Health: Endurance + 5
The amount of physical damage one can withstand
before succumbing to their wounds. When health
reaches 0, the Character is knocked unconscious
and left at the mercy of their enemies. Health is
only recovered through medical attention.

Aura

 Aura: Perception + Willpower + 5
The physical manifestation of the soul. Aura is an extra
layer of protection for its user, and is depleted when
attacks cannot be avoided. One can also attempt to
influence the world around them throught it in the form
of a Semblance.
When Aura reaches 0, PER cannot be added to defensive
rolls and one cannot use their semblance.
Aura is fully recharged by not performing any strenuous
action for 1 hour.

Capacity

 Capacity: Discipline + 1
The amount of ammunition and dust a Huntsman’s or
Huntress’ weapon can hold before needing to reload.
Using Ranged attacks or Dust depletes Capacity.
Characters are considered to have an infinite amount
of ammunition on their person, but a finite amount
of ammunition in a given magazine.
If a Character possesses Dust, only one form is
declared per reload. A Character may choose to use
mundane ammunition as normal instead of the currently
equipped dust.
When Capacity reaches 0, one cannot use ranged attacks
or Dust. Capacity is completely filled every time the character spends an action to reload.






Strength

 Ability to manipulate the world through physical
effort. Used in melee attack rolls for accuracy
and damage and any checks that involve trying
to physically overpower an obstacle.

Agility

 Ability to move quickly or precisely. Used for
ranged weapon accuracy and checks that require
sudden, rapid or precise movement.

Endurance

 Stamina and durability. Determines Health. Used
in checks that involve withstanding a hazard or
maintaining effort over a long period of time.


Perception

 Ability to manipulate the world through physical
effort. Used in melee attack rolls for accuracy
and damage and any checks that involve trying
to physically overpower an obstacle.

Willpower

 Ability to influence the world with one's mind,
and also contributes to Aura, Semblance and mundane
influence, such as lying or bribing.

Discipline

 Study and experience. Determines Capacity and
Complexity of a Character’s weapon. Used for all Dust checks
and checks that require a clear mind and controlled thoughts.
